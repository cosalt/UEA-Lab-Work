Gentle Hatha Yoga

We offer Gentle Hatha yoga, a gentle and meditative style of yoga which focuses on breathing, stretching, and body control. It is ideal for beginners, and particularly those who want to relax and disconnect from everyday stress.

Vinyasa Yoga

We offer Vinyasa Yoga, a fast-paced, fitness-based approach.

Restorative Yoga

We offer Restorative Yoga, a relaxing style of yoga that relives stress and heals.

Iyengar Yoga

We also offer Iyengar Yoga, a precise type of yoga that focuses on balance and alignment. It strengthens ligaments and muscles.
